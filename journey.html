China Study Journey

A step-by-step guide from university application to arriving in China as an international student.

🛫 Application Journey

1️⃣ Choose Your University

Research universities, programs and scholarships.

2️⃣ Submit Application

Upload academic documents and apply online.

3️⃣ Receive Admission Letter

Wait for the university admission result.

4️⃣ Get JW201 / JW202 Form

Required for your student visa application.

5️⃣ Apply for Student Visa

Apply for an X1 or X2 visa at the Chinese Visa Center.

6️⃣ Book Flight & Accommodation

Prepare your travel plans before departure.

7️⃣ Arrive in China

Attend registration and orientation.

8️⃣ Residence Permit

Apply for a residence permit within 30 days.

📄 Documents Checklist

  • ✅ Passport
  • ✅ Academic Transcripts
  • ✅ IELTS Certificate
  • ✅ Recommendation Letters
  • ✅ Passport-size Photos
  • ✅ Physical Examination Form
  • ✅ Bank Statement
  • ✅ Admission Letter

🛂 Student Visa Types

X1 Visa

For degree programs longer than 180 days. Requires a residence permit after arrival.

X2 Visa

For short-term programs under 180 days.

🎒 Before You Arrive

  • 📱 Download WeChat
  • 💳 Install Alipay
  • 💵 Carry Some RMB Cash
  • 📄 Keep Admission Documents Ready
  • 🏫 Contact Your University International Office
  • 📍 Save Your Campus Address Offline

⚠️ Don't Forget

Visa documents

Health insurance

Emergency contacts

University registration details

Common Mistakes

❌ Applying too late

❌ Ignoring scholarship deadlines

❌ Booking flights before visa approval

❌ Not researching living costs
