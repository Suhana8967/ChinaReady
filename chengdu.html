STUDENT LIFE

Living in Chengdu

Chengdu combines modern city life with a relaxed pace, making it one of the most comfortable student cities in China. Students enjoy affordable housing, excellent public transport, famous cuisine and growing internship opportunities.

STUDENT BUDGET

Monthly Student Budget

  • Accommodation: ¥800–1800
  • Food: ¥800–1500
  • Transport: ¥100–250
  • Total: ¥2500–4500+
