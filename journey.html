STUDENT ROADMAP

Your Journey To China

From choosing a university to arriving in China, this guide walks you through every major step.

Application Roadmap

1️⃣ Choose University

Research cities, programs and scholarships.

2️⃣ Prepare Documents

Passport, transcripts, IELTS and recommendation letters.

3️⃣ Submit Applications

Apply to universities and scholarship programs.

4️⃣ Receive Admission

Get admission letter and JW202/JW201 form.

5️⃣ Apply For Visa

Submit documents to the Chinese Visa Center.

6️⃣ Arrive In China

Register at your university and begin studies.
