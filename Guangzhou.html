GUANGZHOU OVERVIEW

Why Students Choose Guangzhou

Guangzhou is one of China's most international cities, offering excellent universities, strong business connections and a multicultural environment. Students benefit from affordable living costs compared to Shenzhen while remaining close to major career opportunities.

STUDENT LIFE

Living in Guangzhou

Guangzhou offers a dynamic mix of modern city life, rich culture and affordable living. Students enjoy excellent transportation, diverse food options and easy access to internships across the Greater Bay Area.
