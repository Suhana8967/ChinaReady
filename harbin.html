HARBIN OVERVIEW

Why Students Choose Harbin

Harbin is a major educational center in Northeast China, known for its engineering universities, affordable living costs and unique blend of Chinese and Russian culture. Students enjoy strong academic programs and one of China's most distinctive city environments.

STUDENT LIFE

Living in Harbin

Harbin offers a unique student experience with affordable housing, efficient transportation and a rich cultural atmosphere. The city is especially famous for its Ice and Snow Festival and beautiful winter scenery.
