Scholarships in China

China offers generous scholarships for international students, covering tuition fees, accommodation and monthly stipends.

🏆 Fully Funded Scholarships

🇨🇳 CSC Scholarship

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is the most popular scholarship for international students.

  • Full Tuition Coverage
  • Free Dormitory
  • Monthly Stipend
  • Medical Insurance

🏙 Provincial Scholarships

Offered by provincial governments such as Jiangsu, Zhejiang and Shanghai.

  • Partial or Full Tuition
  • Easier Competition
  • Available in Many Universities

🎓 University Scholarships

Many Chinese universities offer scholarships directly to international students.

  • Merit-Based Awards
  • Tuition Reduction
  • Freshman Scholarships

⭐ University Scholarships

Zhejiang University Scholarship

Partial to full tuition support.

Beijing Institute of Technology Scholarship

Strong funding opportunities for international students.

XJTLU Scholarship

Merit-based awards and tuition reductions.
