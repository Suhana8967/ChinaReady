SHENZHEN OVERVIEW

Why Students Choose Shenzhen

Shenzhen is one of China's youngest cities and a major technological hub. Known for its engineering universities, historical landmarks and lower living costs, it offers an excellent balance between academic quality and affordability.

STUDENT LIFE

Living in Shenzhen

Shenzhen is China's technology capital and one of the fastest-growing cities in the world. Students enjoy modern infrastructure, excellent public transportation, international communities and unmatched access to technology companies, startups and internships.
