XI'AN OVERVIEW

Why Students Choose Xi'an

Xi'an is one of China's oldest cities and a major educational center. Known for its engineering universities, historical landmarks and lower living costs, it offers an excellent balance between academic quality and affordability.

STUDENT LIFE

Living in Xi'an

Xi'an offers a relaxed student lifestyle with affordable housing, excellent public transport and a rich cultural atmosphere. It is significantly cheaper than Beijing and Shanghai while still providing strong academic opportunities.
