SURVIVAL MANDARIN

Learn Mandarin For Daily Life

You do not need fluent Mandarin before studying in China. Learning a few essential words and phrases can make daily life, transportation, shopping and campus life much easier.

Essential Phrases

👋 Hello

Nǐ hǎo (你好)

🙏 Thank You

Xièxiè (谢谢)

👋 Goodbye

Zàijiàn (再见)

❓ How Much?

Duōshao qián? (多少钱?)

Campus Survival Vocabulary

🏫 Classroom

Jiàoshì (教室)

📚 Library

Túshūguǎn (图书馆)

🍜 Cafeteria

ESSENTIAL APPS

Apps Every Student Should Install

📱 WeChat

Messaging, payments, university groups and daily communication.

💳 Alipay

Mobile payments, transportation and many student services.

📚 Pleco

The most popular Chinese dictionary for international students.

🗺️ Baidu Maps

Useful for navigation, metro routes and local addresses.

🍜 Meituan

Food delivery, discounts and local restaurant deals.

🚕 Didi

China's most popular taxi and ride-hailing app.

🎯 Good News

You do not need fluent Mandarin before arriving in China. Learning 50–100 common words is usually enough to navigate daily life, transportation, shopping and campus activities.
