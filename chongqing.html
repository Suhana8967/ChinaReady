CHONGQING OVERVIEW

Why Students Choose Chongqing

Chongqing is one of China's fastest-growing cities, offering affordable living costs, modern infrastructure, strong universities and unique urban landscapes. Students enjoy a vibrant city life while spending far less than in Beijing or Shanghai.
